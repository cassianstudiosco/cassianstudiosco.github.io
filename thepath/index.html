The Path

Race on a winding forest trail, outsmart hazards, and collect objects to reach the finish first.

The Path is a fast, fun, and portable card-based adventure game - now ported to the iPhone!

Each turn, you draw three cards. You play one on yourself, one on an opponent, and discard the third.

You might jog forward… or get sidetracked by the scenic vista.

You might add a compass to your backpack… or get stuck with poison ivy.

Every card you play is a decision. Every turn is a new fork in the road.

The goal? Be the first to reach the end of the trail — if you can outwit your friends and dodge the hazards along the way.

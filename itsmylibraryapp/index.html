ItsMyLibraryApp

Do you love reading and want a fun, intuitive way to track your reading journey? Whether you’re an avid bookworm, a casual reader, or striving to read more, ItsMyLibraryApp makes every book count.

Key Features

Smart Book Search: Find books instantly using the OpenLibrary API.

Customizable Points: Adjust points-per-page settings, genre multipliers, and activate a speed-reading bonus.

Reading Insights: Track total pages read, points earned, and your reading pace through interactive charts.

Personal Ratings & Comments: Rate finished books and keep notes to remember key insights.

Yearly Reading Challenges: Filter your library by year or custom dates to track progress over time.

Clean, Beautiful Interface: A seamless experience designed exclusively for iOS.

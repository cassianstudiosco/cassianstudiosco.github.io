Privacy Policy

Thank you for playing our games. Protecting your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use the app.

1. No Personal Data Collection

Our apps do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

All gameplay data (such as progress, settings, and unlocks) is stored locally on your device. We do not have access to this data, and it is not transmitted to external servers.

2. Local Device Storage

• Gameplay progress, preferences, and earned items are saved locally on your device

• You are fully in control of your data and may delete it at any time by uninstalling the app

3. Optional Ads Only

Apps may offer optional advertisements, which you can choose to watch to earn in-game rewards.

• Ads are never required to play

• We do not collect personal information for advertising

• Ads, when shown, are provided by third-party ad networks and may follow their own privacy policies

4. No Tracking or Analytics

Our apps do not use third-party analytics, tracking tools, or profiling services. Your gameplay behavior is not tracked across apps or websites.

5. Permissions

The app may request standard device permissions required for proper functionality. These permissions are used only to support gameplay features and are not used to collect personal data.

6. Security

All data remains on your device. We recommend using standard device security measures (such as passcodes or biometric locks) to protect your information.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any updates will be made available within the app.

8. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

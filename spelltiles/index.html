Spell with Tiles

Build words. Place tiles. Master the board.

Spell with Tiles is a strategic word puzzle where every move counts. Place tiles, form words, and clear the board using smart positioning and creative thinking. It’s not just about spelling; it’s about strategy.

How to Play

  • Drag and place tiles on the board
  • Form words horizontally and vertically
  • Clear letters to score points and open space
  • Plan ahead—every placement shapes the game

What Makes It Different

  • Strategy-first gameplay — placement matters as much as words
  • Dynamic boards — every game plays differently
  • Satisfying combos — create multiple words in one move
  • Progressive challenge — improve your skills over time

Special Modes & Challenges

  • Daily Challenges — new puzzles every day to test your skills
  • Word Reveal Mode — uncover hidden words piece by piece
  • Unique mini-game experiences that keep gameplay fresh

Customize Your Experience

  • Create custom backgrounds — design your own visual style
  • 40+ fonts — personalize how your tiles and words appear
  • 600+ stickers — earn, collect, and place stickers your way

Play Your Way

  • Take your time and think through each move
  • Or push for high scores with bold, fast plays
  • Track your progress and sharpen your strategy

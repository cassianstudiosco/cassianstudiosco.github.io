ItsMyBudget

ItsMyBudget is your all-in-one personal finance assistant designed to make budgeting simple and effective. Whether you’re managing household expenses or saving for your next big goal, ItsMyBudget provides the tools you need to take charge of your finances.

Key Features

Customizable Budgets: Set a monthly budget with your preferred start date, currency, and description.

Expense Tracking: Quickly add, edit, or delete expenses with details like date, amount, and description. Use previously entered descriptions for faster entry.

Smart Filters: Filter expenses by date, amount, or description to view exactly what you need.

Dynamic Charts: Gain insights into your spending habits with bar and pie charts. Switch between timeframes like the last 7 days, 8 weeks, or 12 months.

Budget Overview: Instantly see your total spent and remaining budget for the current cycle.

Seamless Organization: Group and visualize smaller expense categories in the pie chart for clarity.

Trend Analysis: Discover trends with a detailed regression line on charts.
